Credentials

Ms. Katherine K. O’Neil, President of KKO, has more than 30 years experience serving the transportation industry. Prior to forming KKO, Ms. O’Neil was Manager of Information Systems for the Boston & Maine Railroad Commuter Service Department and Service Planner for the MBTA. With her extensive training in transportation operations and management information systems, Ms. O’Neil has consulted on the development of many computerized systems. Her knowledge as a computer specialist and her planning expertise plays a key role in the development and completion of transit projects.
