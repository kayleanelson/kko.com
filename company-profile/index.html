Company Profile

KKO and Associates, L.L.C. is a professional services and consulting firm specializing in two disciplines: Information Systems Support and Management Consulting.

Founded in 1984 in Andover, Massachusetts, KKO specializes in serving the needs of public and private transportation operators. Under the leadership of Ms. Katherine K. O’Neil, KKO has been instrumental in the successful completion of a wide variety of transportation projects for public agencies and private sector clients throughout the United States and around the world.

A representative list of service offerings is found below:

  • Management Consulting
    • Procurement Support
    • Strategic Planning
    • Financial Modeling and Analysis
    • Performance Audits
  • Information Systems Support
    • Information Technology Strategy
    • Information Technology Procurement Support
    • Software Applications

KKO brings a unique expertise to Management Consulting for clients in the transportation industry. KKO specializes in the business requirements and operating environments of transportation operators. This perspective allows for a keen ability to tailor solutions for the specialized needs of each individual client.
